STAGE 1) Option preview STAGE 2) Option familiarity & preferences


Stage 2

Choice experiment



1) Below are C.D images with corresponding sound clips. We want to know your familiarity and preference for each option.


2) Look at each C.D cover one at a time and listen to the very short sound clip once. Rate your familiarity and preference for each option using the tick boxes provided before moving on to the next option. Do not spend a long time on each option.


3) By familiarity, we mean your familiarity with the category that the image and music represents. You do not need to know the specific recording of a track for it to be be familiar. You might for instance be very familiar with the artist or composer thus making the option familiar. We expect however that some of the music will be unfamiliar to you. That is not a problem, simply give it a low familiarity rating when asked.


4) By preference, we are simply referring to how much you like it. Do not think too hard about the decision and record your intuitive response.


5) Ensure that you rate each option and that a tick box is selected for every question.


6) At the end of the page, fill in a short questionnaire and then press the green "Submit" button to finish the study
